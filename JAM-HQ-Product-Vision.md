# JAM HQ — Product Vision
*A luxury, AI-native operating system for an extraordinary life.*

> This vision is grounded in what JAM HQ already is — its surfaces (Command, Map, Move, Build, Intelligence), its identity (deep-space dark, aurora glass, the AI orb), and the life it serves (Jay Martinez: movement athlete & coach, co-owner of Obstacle Ninja Academy and Podium Creations, creator @jayy_martinez, husband to Chelsea). It defines where the product goes next, at the highest level.

---

## 1. Product Concept

**Name:** **JAM HQ** — your personal Mission Control.

**Tagline (lead):** *Mission control for an extraordinary life.*
Alternates: *Become your highest self, on purpose.* · *One HQ. Your whole life, handled.*

**Core purpose.** JAM HQ is the single place where you **create, track, learn, and grow** across everything that matters — training, businesses, projects, content, goals, habits, relationships, and self. It turns the chaos of a high-output life into one calm, intelligent cockpit, and pairs you with an AI partner that knows your whole world and helps you become world-class.

**Emotional identity.** It should feel like *stepping into the cockpit of a machine built around you.* Calm, premium, and quietly powerful — the opposite of a noisy to-do app. Three feelings to engineer for:
- **Clarity** — "I know exactly what matters right now."
- **Momentum** — "I can see myself becoming who I want to be."
- **Partnership** — "The best intelligence in the world is in my corner."

The vibe is *Iron Man's JARVIS meets a meditation app* — futuristic and intelligent, but warm, grounded, and human. Not gamified-cheesy; aspirational and real.

---

## 2. Core User Experience

**The open.** A ~2-second cinematic boot: the orb blooms to life, the **JAM HQ** wordmark reveals, and the cockpit assembles itself — cards drift up in a staggered cascade behind a living aurora. You never see a blank load; the app feels like it was already there, waiting.

**The home — Command.** You land in **Command**, the daily cockpit. Top of screen: a warm greeting and your **Alignment score** — a single glowing radial gauge (0–100) that answers "how aligned is today with who I'm becoming?" Below it, the **AI briefing**: a few honest sentences from your intelligence ("You're sharp today. First up: Coach @ ONA at 3. One thought waiting to triage. ▸ The single move that makes today a win: …"), ending in one highest-leverage action with one-tap chips. Then your **mission for today** (the one thing), a **momentum** strip, and a **timeline** merged with your calendar.

**How it feels.** Spacious and unhurried — one clear focal action per screen, deep negative space, restrained color with one accent leading per surface. Everything is reachable one-handed. Any daily interaction completes in under three minutes, mostly taps. The aurora breathes behind the glass; tapping anything gives a soft haptic; finishing something rewards you. It feels *alive, premium, and on your side.*

**Two things are always one tap away, on every screen:** the **+ Capture** button (dump any thought in 5 seconds) and the **AI orb** (talk to your intelligence, hands-free or typed).

## 3. Main Features

JAM HQ organizes everything into five surfaces. Each capability below lives in its natural home.

**Create** *(Build · Map)* — Spin up ideas, plans, projects, content, training programs, and systems. Brand/life folders (ONA, Podium, Movement, @jayy, Wife & I, Self) hold notes + projects. The AI co-creates: outline a program, draft a post, structure a launch, brainstorm angles.

**Track** *(everywhere, rolled into Map)* — Live scores computed from real activity, not manual logging where possible. Skills, goals, habits, fitness, business metrics, learning, and growth each feed a domain score. The **Life Map** shows your whole life as a living orbital ecosystem; the **Balance radar** shows the same eight domains as one shape so imbalance is obvious at a glance.

**Learn** *(Move · Map's Learning Lab)* — Personalized learning paths. For movement: ordered drill progressions with cues, common-fault→fix, and "ready-when" gates. For anything else: book/course/skill tracks with what-to-do-next surfaced by the AI. The system always answers "what's the next rep?"

**Grow** *(Move · Map)* — Identity meters and mastery roadmaps toward world-class. You don't just track tasks — you watch yourself level up across disciplines and domains, with the AI naming your blind spots before they become plateaus.

**Plan** *(Command · Build)* — Daily mission, weekly periodization (training microcycle), project roadmaps with steps/due-dates/next-action, and an Action Center that turns metrics into concrete moves.

**Reflect** *(Map · Intelligence)* — Journaling, automatic **Weekly Reviews** (where your attention actually went, one focus for next week) and a monthly **Upgrade Report** (the app reflects on how you used it and proposes improvements you accept or dismiss — a self-improving loop).

**AI coaching** *(Intelligence + per-surface)* — One AI partner wearing many hats: Chief of Staff, Performance Coach, Creative Director, business ops, Systems Architect. It briefs you, builds your sessions, drafts your content, and thinks through decisions with you — always grounded in your real data.

**Projects** — Real execution objects: ordered checklist steps with progress %, a due date (on-track/soon/overdue), and the **next action surfaced** on the card so you always know the very next move.

**Skills** — Discipline skill trees (Foundation → Developing → Advanced → Elite), each skill with status, %, cue, and tap-to-track drills/fundamentals. A "Working On" rollup shows every current edge in one place.

**Habits** — Lightweight identity-based streaks tied to who you're becoming, feeding domain scores; the AI protects the keystone ones.

**Training** *(Move — the showpiece)* — The Movement OS: identity meters, the movement pyramid, mastery roadmaps, AI **Build-My-Session** (programs today's work from your tree + readiness + blind spots) and **Plan-Week** periodization.

**Business / life systems** *(Build)* — ONA, Podium, and the Creator Studio with live metrics, pipelines, initiatives, and an Action Center; the relevant agent turns numbers into next moves.

## 4. Premium AI Features

These make JAM HQ feel a generation ahead — magical, but genuinely useful because they run on *your* live data.

- **AI daily briefing** — every morning, an honest, specific cockpit brief ending in the single highest-leverage move, with one-tap actions.
- **Adaptive plans** — sessions and weeks that reshape to your readiness, recent work, and what you've been neglecting — never a static template.
- **Smart progress tracking** — scores and trends computed from real activity, so the system "just knows" how you're doing without manual logging.
- **Personalized recommendations** — "you haven't trained pulling in 3 weeks," "Podium's the lever this week," surfaced where you'll act on them.
- **Automatic reflections** — weekly and monthly reviews written for you, surfacing attention imbalance, patterns, and one focus.
- **Goal decomposition** — name a big goal ("standing back tuck," "$30k Podium month") and the AI breaks it into a progression of milestones, drills, and next actions.
- **Skill progression maps** — visual trees with gates that tell you exactly when you've earned the next step.
- **Project copilots** — an AI attached to each project that drafts steps, writes content, and nudges the next action.
- **Voice interaction** — talk to the orb hands-free (mid-training, driving), capture and command by voice.
- **Memory-based coaching** — the Companion remembers your conversations, history, and goals, so it grows with you instead of resetting.
- **Life pattern detection** — quietly learns what you open, ignore, and abandon, and proposes upgrades to your systems (the monthly Upgrade Report).
- **Operator actions** — the AI doesn't just advise; it *acts* — block calendar time, log a session, save a capture, set your focus, draft an email — with you confirming anything irreversible.

## 5. Visual Design Direction

**UI style.** Premium **deep-space dark** with **aurora glass** — translucent frosted cards floating over a near-black canvas, lit by a slow-drifting aurora and fine film grain so nothing looks flat. Subtle **HUD corner-ticks** on key cards give it a "mission control" instrument feel without clutter. Cinematic, calm, high-end.

**Color palette** (on near-black `#0A0B0D`):
- Cyan/blue `#45B7E8` · Teal `#2DD4BF` · Coral `#FF6B5B` · Green `#34D399` · Gold `#E9C46A` · Warm orange `#FF8A4C` / `#F4A261`.
- Text near-white `#F5F5F7`, muted `#8A8A95`, dim `#5A5A66`. Hairline borders (white 6–12%).
- Color is **semantic and restrained**: each surface and domain has one accent; one accent leads per screen; neon used as light, not paint.

**Typography.** A tall condensed display face (**Bebas Neue**) for big numbers, scores, and titles — uppercase, confident. **Inter** for body. **JetBrains Mono** for labels, stats, eyebrows, and tags (small, letter-spaced, uppercase). The number-forward display type makes data feel like instrumentation.

**Layout.** Single-column, mobile-first, generous vertical rhythm. One hero per screen, then supporting cards. Bottom tab bar + floating Capture FAB + AI orb. Bottom **sheets** for all detail/modals.

**Dashboard.** "Glanceable depth" — the top of each screen answers the question in one number or shape (a gauge, a radar, a score); detail expands below or in a sheet. Data is always *visual first* (gauges, radars, sparklines, heatmaps, the orbital map), text second.

**Card system.** Frosted glass, rounded 14–22px, hairline border, soft deep shadow, optional accent glow and corner-ticks. Cards breathe in on load and respond to touch.

**Icon style.** Clean inline stroke icons (Lucide-like), 1.5–2px, consistent grid — calm and modern, never skeuomorphic.

**Spacing.** Roomy and rhythmic — let elements breathe; restraint is the luxury signal.

**Overall vibe.** *Futuristic, cinematic, motivational, high-end.* The cockpit of a calm, intelligent machine built around your life.

## 6. Animation & Interaction Design

Motion is a core feature, not decoration — it should feel **physical, fast, and rewarding.**

- **Boot** — the cinematic orb-bloom + wordmark reveal + lift-away into the cockpit.
- **Page transitions** — surfaces cross-fade; each screen's cards rise in a **staggered cascade** (~35ms apart) so the dashboard assembles itself.
- **Card animations** — soft rise-and-fade on entry; gentle parallax/breathing on hero cards (the aurora and meshes pulse slowly).
- **Progress animations** — gauges sweep to their value, radars draw in, bars and rings fill with eased motion; numbers count up.
- **AI response** — the orb pulses while thinking; replies stream in; action chips appear and flip to **✓ done** when tapped.
- **Dashboard loading** — **skeleton shimmer** placeholders, never spinners or blank states.
- **Gestures** — swipe between surfaces, pull sheets up/down, long-press to capture by voice.
- **Hover/tap** — everything interactive scales ~0.96 on press with a light **haptic**; tactile and instant.
- **Completion moments** — mastering a skill or logging a session triggers a **confetti burst + success haptic**; inbox-zero gets a quiet celebration. Progress should feel *good in the body.*

Principle: motion is quick and weightless (200–340ms), eased, and purposeful — it should make the app feel alive without ever feeling slow. Full reduced-motion support.

## 7. Information Architecture

**Five surfaces** (bottom tab bar), plus two global tools.

1. **Command** — the daily cockpit. Alignment score · AI briefing + actions · today's mission · momentum · timeline/calendar.
2. **Map** — the living Life Map: 8 domains (Athlete, Business, Relationships, Health, Creativity, Learning, Adventure, Growth) as an orbital ecosystem + a Balance radar. Sub-spaces: each domain (Health, Relationships, Learning Lab, Adventure Hub), plus the daily **Inbox/Triage** and **Journal**, **Weekly Review**, **Monthly Upgrade Report**.
3. **Move** — the Movement OS. Identity meters · movement pyramid · skill trees (6 disciplines) with drills/fundamentals/gates · Build-My-Session · Plan-Week · "Working On" rollup · body radar.
4. **Build** — businesses & creation. ONA HQ · Podium · Creator Studio · brand/life folders with projects · Action Center.
5. **Intelligence** — the AI hub: the Companion (whole-app context, memory, operator actions) and specialist agents (Chief, Coach, Creative, Ops, Architect).

**Global, on every screen:** **+ Capture** (→ Inbox) and the **AI orb** (→ Intelligence). Universal capture means a thought anywhere lands in one inbox and routes later.

**How it connects:** capture feeds the Inbox → triage routes to domains/projects → activity feeds domain scores → scores feed the Map and Alignment → the AI reads all of it to brief, plan, coach, and act. One graph, many views.

## 8. Daily Use Flow

**Morning (2–3 min).** Open → cinematic boot → **Command**. Read the AI briefing, glance the Alignment gauge, confirm/adjust today's mission, tap one prefilled action (block prep time, triage 2 thoughts). You leave knowing the single thing that makes today a win.

**During the day (seconds at a time).** Capture thoughts with **+** as they hit (mid-training, mid-drive). Before training, **Move → Build My Session** for a plan from your tree, readiness, and blind spots; log it after with a confetti hit. Talk to the **orb** when you need to think, learn, or decide — and let it act (block time, save a draft, log the session). Check **Build** when a business number needs a move.

**Night (5–10 min).** Triage the Inbox to zero. Journal a few lines. Glance the Map to see the day's effect on your domains. On Sundays, the **Weekly Review** writes itself — set one focus for next week and one-tap schedule it. Monthly, the **Upgrade Report** proposes how to run your life and the app better; you accept or dismiss. Close knowing tomorrow's already shaped.

## 9. MVP Version (powerful but realistic)

Ship the smallest version that already feels magical. Much of this exists; the MVP is making these five loops *great*:

1. **Command cockpit** — Alignment gauge + AI daily briefing with one-tap actions + today's mission + momentum.
2. **Universal Capture → Inbox → triage** — the frictionless keystone; nothing gets lost.
3. **Move (Movement OS, lite)** — skill trees with drills/gates + Build-My-Session (AI, with smart fallback) + session logging.
4. **The Companion** — one always-present AI with whole-app context, memory, and operator actions (calendar/session/capture/focus).
5. **Weekly Review + Life Map** — the reflection loop and the at-a-glance balance picture.

Plus the premium shell: cinematic boot, aurora ambience, staggered motion, haptics, reward moments, bottom sheets, dark aurora-glass design system. Cloud sync + auth so it's truly yours across devices. **Definition of MVP success:** for two weeks straight, you open it first thing every morning and it tells you something true and useful you'd have missed.

## 10. Future "God-Tier" Version

Where JAM HQ goes at its highest potential:

- **A truly agentic intelligence** — proactively plans your week, drafts your content calendar, prepares your coaching sessions, and queues moves for your businesses; you approve, it executes.
- **Ambient + voice-first** — a JARVIS-grade voice partner you talk to hands-free all day; it briefs you on a walk, takes captures, and coaches mid-session.
- **Deep integrations** — wearables (Oura/Whoop/Apple Health) auto-fill readiness; Stripe/Square/GymDesk stream live business numbers; calendar/email/Drive flow in and out — no manual entry.
- **Predictive coaching** — pattern detection that forecasts plateaus, burnout, and slumps before they hit, and intervenes early.
- **Vision & media** — film a rep, get instant form analysis against the elite model; auto-edit and schedule your content.
- **Self-evolving app** — the monthly Upgrade Report becomes real: with your approval, JAM HQ reshapes its own dashboards and automations around how you actually live, versioning itself v1 → v2 → v3.
- **Legacy view** — a long-horizon "future self" mode that makes decisions against the person you're becoming over years, not just today.

The end state: an operating system that quietly runs the operational layer of your life and businesses, keeps you progressing toward world-class, and hands you — every morning — a life that feels *chosen* rather than reacted to.
